Differences between Sample past tense and Present perfect tense

Defination of both tenses

Sample past tense

Present perfect tense

Sample present tense express an action which happened at a specific time in the past it describes how and when the action happened in the past. it does not have any connection with present

Present perfect tense express an action which happened in the past but still continue up to now or an action which recently finished

Main differences between Sample past tense and Present perfect tense :

  1. we use sample past tense to show an action which happen happend in the past and have no connection with present
    Examples: I went to store yesterday
    it means that i went out for shoping yesterday and I finished my shoping
  2. We can use both the past tense and the present perfect tense to talk about things in the past
    • I went to China
    • Did you go to China?
    • I have been to China
    • Have you been to china?
  3. We cannot use a specific time with the present perfect tense.
    Examples:
    • I went to China in 2007
    • Did you go to china in 2007?
  4. We use present perfect to talk about experiences
    Examples:
    • I have tried raw fish
    • she has visited many contries
    • They have been here before
  5. We cannot use specific time in present perfect tense but we can tell how many times
    Examples:
    • I visited austrilia two times in 2015
    • She called 3 times while you were in the shower
